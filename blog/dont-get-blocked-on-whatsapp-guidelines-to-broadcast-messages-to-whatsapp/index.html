DONT GET BLOCKED ON WHATSAPP: Guidelines to broadcast messages to WhatsApp

Business messaging is now a vital and esteemed aspect of the customer journey as more than one out of two customers are preferring businesses to use modern channels to communicate with them, with 53% of customers are interested to engage with a business where they can message directly. Considering the fact, many businesses are starting to adapt to WhatsApp as a channel for customer communication.

Regardless of whether your WhatsApp account is blocked by a customer or by WhatsApp itself, the reasons are regularly down to either breaking WhatsApp’s terms of service or a complaint of customer lead by poor user experience. Whatever the reason, getting blocked on WhatsApp will be a nightmare for any business.

Following the mentioned guidelines will ensure your WhatsApp account will not get blocked.

Let customers save your WhatsApp number

In the event when customers are saving your business/individual WhatsApp number in their telephone contacts at that point blocking chances are significantly less. So consistently urge customers to save your WhatsApp number.

OPT-IN

You should send messages only to customers who have OPT-IN to get business messages from your WhatsApp number. It will be ideal for a business to develop a subscription list of OPT-IN by communicating through email or text messages and requesting them to OPT-IN. Businesses should also give an option to OPT-OUT from messaging list to customers if they feel like to unsubscribe from the list by using keywords like “UNSUB” “OPTOUT” “STOP” etc.

NO SPAMMING

Do not send irrelevant and frequent messages to your customers just to increase sale and also, avoid sending messages between 9 pm to 9 am. This can irritate customers and can lead to spam reporting to WhatsApp and then blocking.

2-way Interactions

The easiest way to avoid number blocking on WhatsApp by customers is through interactions. A business should always put the best efforts to develop a 2-way communication channel with its subscriber’s list, particularly if the beneficiary answers back to your message or if the discussion started by the customer.

Integrate WhatsApp business account

A business should migrate from a personal WhatsApp account and should integrate WhatsApp Business account as a channel for communication with its customers as a blocking algorithm of WhatsApp business is different from WhatsApp personal account.

Please note WhatsApp blocking algorithm get updated time to time and so practices mentioned in this article explains only the best practice to avoid blocking.
